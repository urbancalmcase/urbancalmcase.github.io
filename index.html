Urban Calm Case

Prepared, not panicked – resilience for modern life

Why Urban Calm Case?

Urban life is busy and beautiful – until something unexpected happens. Power cuts, supply disruptions or civil unrest can leave you and your loved ones vulnerable. Urban Calm Case is a curated resilience system that keeps you informed, warm, connected and calm. It isn't about fear; it's about preparedness. Our kit is designed using real-world lessons from conflict zones and packaged in a discreet, elegant Pelican hard case.

What's inside

  • Pelican 1510/1535 hard case with modular dividers
  • USB‑C rechargeable headlamp and compact flashlight
  • Two high‑capacity power banks – keep devices running
  • Optional foldable solar panel for off‑grid charging
  • Hand‑crank/battery radio with earphones for news and updates
  • Collapsible water containers & purification tablets
  • Medical essentials: pressure bandage, tourniquet, trauma shears and basics
  • Thermal blanket, wool socks & beanie for warmth indoors
  • Work gloves and compact pry tool
  • Notebook, pen and document pouch for vital information
  • Comfort touches: tea/coffee sachets to lift spirits
